# Open Downtown Store

> **tl;dr:** Launch date: 0612. This estimate is achievable if the team protects store readiness as fixed-time `Must` scope and treats nonessential merchandising polish as variable scope. The plan is directionally solid, but permit approval remains the main schedule risk.

## Estimate Summary

- **Project MMDD:** 0612.
- **Planning mode:** Fixed launch date.
- **Fixed time, variable scope:** Hold 0612 and cut secondary merchandising and launch-event extras before moving the date.
- **Must-have scope:** The store can open safely with inventory on shelves, staff trained, payments working, and required permits approved.

## Milestones

- **Milestone 1 (Must): Lock must-have scope on 0422.** Confirm permits, staffing plan, opening inventory, and the explicit items that can wait until after launch.
- **Milestone 2 (Must): Store setup complete on 0513.** Fixtures, signage, point-of-sale hardware, and back-room operations are ready for trial runs.
- **Milestone 3 (Must): Staff training and soft opening on 0527.** Run a limited opening to validate staffing, checkout flow, and daily operating rhythm.
- **Milestone 4 (Should): Neighborhood preview event on 0603.** Invite a small audience to test demand and smooth out remaining customer experience issues.
- **Milestone 5 (Must): Open to the public on 0612.** Launch regular store hours with the agreed must-have experience in place.

## Risks and Scope Tradeoffs

- **Permit approval could delay the opening.** If final approval slips past 0603, cut preview-event scope and preserve the public opening checklist.
- **Inventory timing is still uncertain.** Prioritize core products first and hold secondary merchandising ideas for after launch if deliveries slip.
